Legal, Privacy & Ethics

Because dignity is non-negotiable. Below are the standards that keep JAOLA safe, compliant, and trauma-aware — for us and for you.

HIPAA & Confidentiality

JAOLA follows HIPAA-aligned principles. We do not share health data without your written consent. Client records are encrypted, secured, and never sold — period.

Privacy Policy

  • Emails/forms are used for direct support only.
  • No info is ever sold or used for advertising.
  • We use secure platforms like Gmail & Formspree with locked access.
  • We delete what you ask us to delete. You’re in control.

Disclaimer

JAOLA is not a law firm or clinic. We provide support, not legal advice or emergency medical treatment. We guide, document, and clarify — not diagnose or represent.

Terms of Use

  • Respectful, honest communication is expected.
  • Service may be paused or ended if safety boundaries are crossed.
  • We rely on full, accurate info to advocate effectively.

Acknowledgements

We review our ethics monthly. We update for clarity, legality, and trauma-aware standards. Last updated: May 2025.
