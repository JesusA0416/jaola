Legal, Privacy & Ethics

Because dignity is non-negotiable. JAOLA is built to protect what matters — your records, your trust, and your time.

Our Commitments

JAOLA protects every client interaction with real structure, real privacy, and real standards. Here's how.

HIPAA & Confidentiality

We align with HIPAA-compliant practices. Your health and intake information is kept confidential and never shared without written consent. We use encrypted platforms and locked access only.

Privacy Policy

  • No client information is sold or used for advertising.
  • Email, intake forms, and service data are used for support only.
  • We delete records upon request. You’re in control.
  • We use Gmail and Formspree, which use secure HTTPS encryption.

Disclaimer

JAOLA is not a legal firm or licensed clinic. We offer support, not legal representation or medical treatment. You still deserve clarity — just not courtroom litigation from us.

Terms of Use

  • All clients must communicate respectfully and honestly.
  • We may pause or discontinue services if boundaries are crossed.
  • We can only work with accurate and complete client data.

Policy Updates

We review and revise our ethics and privacy terms monthly. If policies change, you’ll know.
