Legal, Privacy & Ethics

Because dignity is non-negotiable. Below are the standards that keep JAOLA safe, compliant, and trauma-aware — for us and for you.

HIPAA & Confidentiality

JAOLA adheres to HIPAA principles and never shares client health data without written consent, unless required by law. Personal records, care notes, and communication logs are stored securely and never sold or disclosed to third parties.

Privacy Policy

  • Emails and messages are used solely for communication and support.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade client information — ever.
  • Submission forms and intake data are stored securely and reviewed only by authorized support staff.
  • External platforms (e.g., Gmail, Formspree) are used with secure connections and reviewed regularly for privacy compliance.

Disclaimer

JAOLA is not a law firm, medical clinic, or emergency response unit. We provide documentation assistance, housing education, CNA-level care, and trauma-informed guidance — but not legal representation or clinical diagnosis.

We’re here to help — but we are not a substitute for licensed attorneys, social workers, or healthcare providers.

Terms of Use

  • Clients agree to communicate respectfully and participate in service planning.
  • We reserve the right to refuse or discontinue services if boundaries, safety, or ethical standards are violated.
  • JAOLA is not responsible for outcomes resulting from incomplete forms, false information, or uncooperative landlords/providers.

Acknowledgements

Our policies are reviewed monthly and may update to reflect new guidelines or regulations. We believe accountability is a form of care, and transparency is part of safety.
