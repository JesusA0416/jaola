What We Do

Housing Services

JAOLA supports tenants, landlords, and housing-insecure clients with trauma-informed, documentation-focused support. We offer:

  • Internal property management
  • Lease and notice interpretation
  • Eviction documentation prep
  • New tenant onboarding
  • Compliance consulting for ethical landlords

Our services are designed to reduce confusion, build clarity, and close gaps in housing access — without delay, judgment, or condescension.

Care Services

We are CNA-certified and licensed to provide:

  • Non-medical in-home support
  • Assistance with hygiene, meals, and transitions
  • Medicaid paperwork and referrals
  • Safe, affirming care for isolated or disabled clients

We provide structure, trust, and boundaries — all documented and delivered with professionalism.

Why JAOLA?

Because this work can’t be learned in a workshop.

JAOLA is not a referral form. It’s not a number on a caseworker’s desk. It’s a fully operational agency — built by someone who knows the difference between helping and hovering.

We show up:

  • On time
  • With documentation
  • With lived experience, clinical training, and legal clarity
  • Without overpromising, overspeaking, or disappearing

We don’t ask you to explain why it’s urgent. We understand that if you’re contacting us — it already is.

JAOLA works in the real world. That means:

  • Leases get read
  • Clients get written instructions
  • Everything is saved, logged, and sent with consent
  • Boundaries are honored
  • Care is scheduled — not requested endlessly

Who We Serve

JAOLA is built for:

  • Clients overwhelmed by paperwork
  • Renters facing legal threats with no advocate
  • Caregivers who can’t be two places at once
  • People who deserve service but are used to being told to “wait”

We also partner with case managers, clinics, first-time landlords, legal aid groups, discharge nurses, and domestic violence organizations.

How We Work

  • Trauma-aware, not trauma-centered
  • Services grounded in documentation, compliance, and transparency
  • Clinical skill meets administrative rigor
  • Bilingual when needed
  • Always with follow-up

We are not a hotline. We are not a one-time call. We are infrastructure — scaled down to serve humans.

If you’re not sure what to ask for — ask anyway.

If you’ve been denied support, we’ll help you figure out why.

If you’ve been carrying it alone, let us carry the part we can.
