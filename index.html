About JAOLA

JAOLA is a Utah-based startup focused on transforming housing and healthcare access. From managing properties to developing home health operations, we’re building safer homes and stronger communities — one service at a time.

Services

Currently Offering

  • Private property oversight (internal portfolio only)
  • Lease and legal form coordination
  • Housing coordination (pilot)
  • Healthcare & compliance research

In Progress

  • Licensed Property Management (Utah)
  • Home Health Assistant Services
  • JAOLA Wellness Housing (2026)

Roadmap

2025: Launch brand, finalize real estate licensure, pilot admin & care coordination services.

2026: Open first wellness housing unit, hire licensed medical & real estate staff, expand offerings across Utah.

Meet the Founder

Founded by Jesús Lopez Aguilar, JAOLA was built to challenge the systems that gatekeep care and stability. With experience in both healthcare and housing coordination, they’re not just building a business — they’re building a movement.
