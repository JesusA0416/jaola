What is JAOLA?

JAOLA is a Utah-based care and housing startup founded to challenge how systems serve the most vulnerable. We're not just a business — we're a blueprint for a better model of dignity, safety, and sustainability.

We operate at the intersection of housing access, home health, and community empowerment — while developing our legal, medical, and real estate infrastructure for long-term expansion.

Our Mission

To build spaces — physical and emotional — where people feel safe, seen, and supported. Whether it’s overseeing a rental property, helping someone navigate care options, or designing future wellness housing — JAOLA stands for accountability, stability, and heart-led action.
