JAOLA provides trauma-aware care and housing support to individuals, families, and those navigating systems that weren’t built for them.

Explore Services

Why JAOLA?

JAOLA isn’t a referral line. It’s a functioning support structure built by someone who knows what being overlooked feels like.

We show up when systems say “come back later.” We stay when other agencies disappear. We explain what others gatekeep. And we document everything — so nothing gets lost, and no one gets dismissed.

  • Housing clarity, not confusion. We walk tenants through rental agreements, notices, evictions, and lease terms with bilingual, trauma-aware explanations.
  • Landlord accountability. JAOLA manages properties and consults new landlords on ethical practices, documentation, and anti-discrimination compliance.
  • Forms, notices, and proof — done right. We help draft, complete, and organize housing documents to ensure they're legally sound and court-ready.
  • Care that’s licensed, bounded, and real. CNA-certified support delivered in-home with clear expectations and documented follow-through.
  • Follow-through is built in. Clients always leave with next steps, deadlines, and direct contact. There’s no “we’ll get back to you.”

JAOLA isn’t charity. It’s structural care for people who’ve been left to build everything alone. If you need clarity, care, and consistency — we’re already ready.
