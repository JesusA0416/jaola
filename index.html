Why JAOLA?

JAOLA isn’t a hotline. It’s not a folder of forgotten referrals. It’s a functioning care and housing service model — built by someone who knows what it feels like to fall through every crack.

We exist because real people are trying to survive stacked barriers. A lease written in legal jargon. A caseworker who doesn't respond. A family member who needs daily help while paperwork sits untouched. JAOLA was created to meet people at that breaking point — and to offer actual structure in place of chaos.

  • Housing clarity, not confusion. We walk tenants through rental agreements, notices, evictions, and lease terms — with patient, bilingual, trauma-aware explanations.
  • Landlord accountability. JAOLA manages properties and consults with new landlords to implement equitable, legally sound practices.
  • Forms, notices, and proof — done right. We draft, organize, and submit housing paperwork with compliance in mind.
  • Care that’s licensed, bounded, and real. CNA-certified in-home support with clear care plans and consented documentation.
  • Follow-through is built in. Written next steps, appointment tracking, and support plans. No “we’ll see.”

JAOLA is infrastructure — housing + care + documentation — built to hold what other agencies drop.
